About Entropy

Building the future of academic management.

Who We Are

Entropy is an innovative team dedicated to transforming academic administration through technology. Our mission is to help educational institutions streamline operations, centralize information, and improve collaboration across campuses.

Our Project

In partnership with Novacampus Alliance, a rapidly growing higher education group, we designed a modern Educational Management System (ENT) capable of supporting multi-campus operations and digital transformation.

  • Centralized multi-campus management
  • Digitalized academic and administrative processes
  • Reliable academic traceability
  • Strategic reporting and analytics
  • Integrated AI-powered assistance

The Challenge

Novacampus Alliance experienced rapid growth over the past six years, doubling its student population, opening new campuses, and diversifying its academic programs. However, its information systems evolved independently, creating operational inefficiencies.

  • Room scheduling conflicts
  • Delayed grade consolidation
  • Billing and payment tracking issues
  • Scattered reporting systems
  • Disconnected management tools

Our Vision

We believe educational institutions deserve a unified ecosystem where students, teachers, administrators, and management teams can work efficiently using a single source of truth.

By combining automation, analytics, and artificial intelligence, Entropy aims to help educational organizations move from fragmented processes to data-driven decision making.

AI Innovation

As part of the project, we introduced the concept of an intelligent AI assistant capable of supporting academic and administrative workflows. The goal is to automate repetitive tasks, improve productivity, and provide faster access to information across the entire organization.
